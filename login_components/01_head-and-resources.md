# Head & External Resources

- **Purpose:** Page metadata and external resources used by the login page.

## Content
- `charset="UTF-8"` and `viewport` meta for responsive layout.
- `<title>Login</title>`
- External CSS: Bootstrap CDN (`https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/npm/bootstrap@5.3.3/dist/css/bootstrap.min.css`).
- Google Font: Barlow (`https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Barlow:wght@400;500;600;700&display=swap`).

## Notes
- Keep the viewport meta for mobile friendliness.
- External resources are loaded from trusted CDNs; consider local copies for offline or internal deployments.
